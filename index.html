Welcome

Neferneferuaten Nefertiti, a historical icon, and an ancient Egyptian queen, ruled in the 18th dynasty of Egypt, as the Grand Royal Wife of the Pharaoh Akhenaten. Nefertiti and her husband were known for a religious revolution, in which they worshipped one god only, Aten, or the sun disc. Nefertiti had many titles including: Hereditary Princess ,Great of Praises, Lady of Grace,Sweet of Love, Lady of The Two Lands, Main King's Wife, Great King's Wife, his beloved, Lady of All Women, and Mistress of Upper and Lower Egypt.
